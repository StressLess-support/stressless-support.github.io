Privacy Policy

StressLess respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy informs you about how we collect, use, disclose, transfer, and store your information when you use our mobile application and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information we collect

We collect some data when you use the app, including:

  • Personal Data: such as your name, email address, in addition to the events you create on the app (tasks are only created locally).
  • Device Information: such as your device's unique identifier, IP address, operating system, and mobile network information.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Identify you when logging in.
  • Personalizing your experience and delivering customized content.
  • Communicating with you, such as responding to your inquiries and sending important updates.

3. Data sharing and disclosure

We do not share your data with third parties.

4. Data retention

We will retain your personal data as long as your account is active. If your account is deleted, all your data will be deleted immediately. You can at any point in time contact our support to see all your saved data and ask for it to be deleted immediately.

5. Your rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data under the GDPR, including the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your information.
  • Object to the processing of your data.
  • Restrict the processing of your data.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

6. Security measures

We take the security of your personal data seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We encrypt data transmissions between your device and our servers using industry-standard protocols.
  • Access Controls: Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and retain the minimum amount of personal data necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.
  • Employee Training: Our employees undergo regular training on data protection and privacy best practices.

While we take these measures to safeguard your information, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at mobile.app.stressless@gmail.com.
